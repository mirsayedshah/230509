Appendix A

Simulating NOx emissions with an online simulator:

The proposed online simulator enables the assessment of system performance and NOx emission levels by employing a response optimization algorithm for various scenarios and input parameter values. This simulator calculates the power plant's gas emissions level using a specially designed optimization model architecture, monitoring alterations in system output in response to changes in environmental factors and input variables.

Simulator link: https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://raw.githubusercontent.com/mirsayedshah/230509/main/online_simulator_for_nox_emissions_calculation.html
